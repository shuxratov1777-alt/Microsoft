About the Company

Founded on April 7, 1975, by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, Microsoft is an American multinational technology corporation headquartered in Redmond, Washington. It is best known for its software products, including the Windows line of operating systems, the Microsoft 365 suite of productivity applications, and the Edge web browser.

Today, Microsoft is a leader in cloud computing, artificial intelligence, gaming, and enterprise hardware, consistently ranking among the most valuable publicly traded companies in the world.

Core Innovations & Products

Windows

The world's leading desktop operating system, providing the foundation for personal and enterprise computing worldwide.

Azure

A comprehensive cloud computing platform offering over 200 products and cloud services designed to build, run, and manage applications.

Microsoft 365

The productivity cloud designed to help everyone achieve what matters, combining best-in-class apps like Word, Excel, and Teams.

Xbox

Microsoft's premier gaming brand, encompassing video game consoles, applications, streaming services, and the Xbox Network.
